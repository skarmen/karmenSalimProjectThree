taskick

Need to organize your to do lists? taskick-it

taskick is a simple productivity app which will help you manage tasks and keep track of accomplishments! taskick helps you organize your tasks in separate cards so you know exactly what to do.

How to taskick?

Organize your tasks in a few clicks:

  1. Create a new task card by clicking on the "Add" button below.
  2. Enter a title for your card (e.g "Work", "Exercise"..).
  3. Start adding tasks to your card. Type your to do item and click the "Add" button.
  4. Keep track of your tasks by checking off items, removing items and clearing the entire card.
  5. Let's taskick-it!
